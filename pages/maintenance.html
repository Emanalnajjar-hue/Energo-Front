Your Trust Matters: Frequently Asked Questions

Q1: How long does the maintenance process take?
Usually, routine maintenance takes between 2 to 4 hours, depending on the nature of the issue.
Q2: Do you provide spare parts, and are they under warranty?
Yes, we provide original and warranted spare parts, and we offer a 3-month warranty on all repairs.
Q3: Is there an inspection fee if I decide not to repair?
Yes, there is a small inspection fee to cover the technician's travel, which is waived if you proceed with the repair.
Q4: Can your technicians fix heavy-duty generators on-site?
Yes, our technicians are equipped to handle most repairs on-site. However, if the issue is complex or requires heavy machinery, we will safely transport it to our specialized workshop.
